# Moderation and Appeals (Draft v1)

## Goals

- Keep participation safe and constructive.
- Make moderation actions understandable and reviewable.

## Moderation action record

Any significant moderation action should record:

- what happened
- policy basis
- action taken
- duration (if temporary)

## Appeals process

- Appeal window: within 14 days of action.
- Appeal venue: public issue unless safety/privacy requires restricted handling.
- Reviewers: at least 2 admins not directly involved in the original action.
- Decision target: respond within 7 days.

## Possible appeal outcomes

- uphold action
- modify action
- reverse action
- request additional facts before final outcome

## Transparency note

When details cannot be public, publish a redacted summary with rationale.
