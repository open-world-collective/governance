# Fund Approval Policy (v1)

## Scope

Applies to all project funds, reimbursements, and vendor payments.

## Required pre-approval

Before spending, create a public issue with:

- purpose and expected outcome
- amount and currency
- payee or category
- timing
- alternatives considered (if material)

## Approval rule

- Minimum 2-admin signoff is required.
- No self-approval: requestor cannot be the only approving admin.
- Approval comments must be explicit (`Approved`) and linked from the decision log.

## Execution and reporting

After payment or reimbursement:

- link the final transaction record
- note variance from planned amount
- capture any follow-up obligations

## Emergency spend

Emergency spend may proceed with 2-admin signoff in an expedited thread, followed by a retrospective log entry within 7 days.
