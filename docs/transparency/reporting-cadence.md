# Transparency Reporting Cadence

## Monthly report

- Frequency: once per month.
- Target publish date: by the 7th day of the following month.
- Template: `docs/transparency/2026-03-report-template.md` (or latest monthly variant).

## Required sections

- decisions made and their IDs
- funding movements and links
- policy changes merged
- open risks and active mitigations

## Quarterly review

Each quarter, include a brief retrospective:

- what governance processes are working
- where process friction exists
- concrete adjustments proposed for next quarter
