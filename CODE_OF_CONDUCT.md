# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, demand clear evidence.
- Critique ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warning.

## Reporting

Report concerns to `hello@open-world-collective.com` with subject: `conduct-report`.

## Enforcement

Admins may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors for severe or repeated violations.
